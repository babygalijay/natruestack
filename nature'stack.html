About NatureStack

NatureStack is the herbs & spices wing of AkunaFarms. We focus on cultivating, drying, blending, and distributing natural herbs, spices, and wellness products grown without synthetic chemicals. Our mission is to bring freshness, wellness, and purity to every home.

  • Fresh & dried herbs and spices
  • Mixed spices: curry, all-purpose, jollof, beef & chicken
  • Herbal teas and natural health drinks
  • Essential oils and hair boosters
